About
RESPLANDOR FESTIVAL

Resplandor 2024, BVEC’s 5th edition of the Techno-Cultural fest, is the eagerly awaited event that ignites excitement far and wide. Spanning four days, it’s a euphoric celebration of creativity themed ‘A Rivertopian Dream.’ It’s a place where ideas blossom and genius thrives, all set against a backdrop of artistic expression. With a legacy of high standards, Resplandor invites you to experience the pinnacle of talent and innovation.
